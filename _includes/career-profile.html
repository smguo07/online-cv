About Me

Hello! My name is Guo Suiming and has been studying in Tsinghua University (as an undergraduate and master student) and the Chinese University of Hong Kong (as a PhD student).

I am interested in the Internet-related industry, as well as the academia, and my research interests are:

-- Big data mining, analysis, modelling

-- Ubiquitous computing

-- City-level data mining and smart cities

-- Urban computing

-- Computer networking

I like doing researches based on real problems from the industry, in a data-analytical methodology.

I am currently an Associate Professor in College of Information Science and Technology and College of Cyber Security, Jinan University (暨南大学), Guangzhou, Guangdong, China (since Fall, 2018).
